How to Use

Adding Subjects:

Enter subject name, grade, and credit hours. Grades can be entered as numbers (10-0) or letters (A+, A, B, C, D, P, F).

Grading Systems:

  • 10 Point Scale: 10 (A+) to 0 (F)
  • 4.0 Scale: 4.0 (A) to 0.0 (F)
  • Percentage: 100% to 0%

Target Calculator:

Enter your desired CGPA and remaining credits to see what grades you need to achieve your goal.

Data Management:

Save semesters, import/export data, and track your progress over time.
